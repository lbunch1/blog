Let me start by pointing out that I am not a writer by any stretch of the imagination. I am simply starting this blog as a subject to practice what I have learned in the world of web development. Where is this going?

My plans for this blog is for it to grow along with my knowledge as well as document my progress. Style and functionality should change either slightly or significantly with each additional post. I hope to transform this to a basic cms as soon as I learn how to access and manipulate a database with nodejs. I am currently making my way through The Odin Project and by the end creating a simple cms should be no problem.
